About Me

Born and brought up in Hyderabad, India, I moved to the US in 2012 to pursue my Masters in Computer Engineering at the University of Southern California. I currently live in Tennessee with my wife and two sons.

I enjoy learning Sanskrit, biking, playing with my kids, and traveling. I'm a spiritual seeker and practice daily Isha Yoga practices. I also volunteer at the Isha Foundation.

I'm pursing a Sanskrit certificate course from Samskrita Bharati. I have two more years to go to complete the course. I'm comfortable making short conversations in Sanskrit and I'm just starting to make an attempt at understanding texts like Bhagavad Gita and Valmiki Ramayana in their original Sanskrit form.
